Curd Rice

Description

Curd Rice is a south indian comfort food. As it is made from curd, it is good for the guts. It is usually paired with pickles. It is the last course in lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup curd
  • salt
  • 1 teaspoon oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 dried red chilli
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 pinch asafoetida
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 green chilli

Steps

  1. Wash Rice and add it to pressure cooker with 1 1/2 cup water and salt. Cook for 3 whistles.
  2. Add curd to cooled rice and mix well. Add salt to taste.
  3. Add coriander leaves
  4. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin and mustard. When splattering begins, add red chill, chana dal and urad dal. Fry till golden
  5. Add ginger, green chilli and curry leaves. After leaves become crisp, add asafoetida.
  6. Pour the tempering onto curd rice and mix.
  7. Garnish with pomegranate seeds
